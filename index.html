About

Often, the written content within the book is the main focus, that the structure of the book itself goes unnoticed. Details such as the materiality of the book, the size, the way the book is held and interacted with are taken into consideration when designing a book. What interested me was the similarities of the book structure within it's genre. Some genres are printed simiarly despite having different publishers, while some publishers also print their books the same size despite it being different genres.

This site was created to document relationships between genres or publishers that I've noticed from observation in book stores. I would walk around each section and if a size similarity was noticed, I would document the size and its format, making the structure of the book the main focus.

Penguin Classics

Dimensions: 13 x 19.6 cm

Despite the books being from different genre's, Penguin Classic books are printed in the same size with the same type of cover and paper. Although, all of these books printed are already classics which already have their values established as they've stayed relevant through time. The structure of the books can be printed similarly as they don't need marketing or recognition through unique design.
